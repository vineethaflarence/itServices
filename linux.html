Setting up 8021.X for Wireless on Linux

Contributed By: Nehal J Wani

First, download the IIITH RADIUS CA Cert from: http://radius.iiit.ac.in/IIIT-H_RADIUS_CA.pem and keep it at a known location.

Connect to the Wireless SSID: wifi@iiith

Make sure you select the options as shown below. Authentication: PEAP, PEAP Version: Automatic, Inner Authentication: MACHAPv2, select the CA Certificate and point it to the one downloaded in the previous step. Enter your 802.1X credentials and then click on "Connect".

Done!!
