How do I install Office 365 on a Mac?

Access and Publisher are not included in Office 365 for Mac.

  • Sign in here  with your IIITH credentialsOnce signed in, it will take you to a new page. On that page, click Install Office towards the top right-hand corner of the screen and then select Office 365 apps to begin downloading Office 365.
  • Once the download has completed, double-click the file you downloaded and follow the onscreen instructions to complete the installation.
  • Open Word, click Get started, and follow the onscreen instructions.
  • Click Sign In, type in your IITH email address, and then sign in to IIITH.
  • Click&Start Using Word to complete the Office 365 activation.
