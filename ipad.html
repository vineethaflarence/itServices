Setting up 802.1X for Wireless on iPad (iOS)

Contributed By: Nehal J Wani, J N Venkatesh

Connect to the wireless network SSID:wifi@iiith

Enter your 802.1X Credentials

You will be shown a non-trusted Certificate. Make sure that the name is "IIIT-H Radius Server Certificate". You can view the certificate by clicking on 'More Details' and match it with: http://radius.iiit.ac.in/IIIT-H_RADIUS_CA.pem . Once satisfied, click on 'Trust' on the upper right corner.

Done!
