Setting up 802.1X for Wireless on MacBook (OS X)

Connect to the wireless network SSID:wifi@iiith

Enter your 802.1X Credentials

You will be asked to examine the server certificate. Make sure that the name is "IIIT-H Radius Server Certificate". You can view the certificate and match it with: http://radius.iiit.ac.in/IIIT-H_RADIUS_CA.pem then click on 'continue'.

You will be asked to confirm addition of the certificate to the trust store. Enter your account credentials to continue.

Done!
